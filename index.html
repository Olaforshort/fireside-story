It was a dark and stormy night...
When a earthquake shook the entire east coast of the United States.
The ground shook viciously, sirens blared from every device in the house, then suddenly every thing went still.
The stillness lasted but a moment as every branch of law enforcement sprung into action, but only two brave souls could possibly get to the bottom of this phenomema: Special Agents Ola & Lily.
But where could they be found?
